Article 44: General principle for transfers
Any transfer of personal data to a third country or international organisation shall take place only if the conditions laid down in this Regulation are complied with by the controller and processor.

Article 45: Transfers on the basis of an adequacy decision
A transfer of personal data to a third country may take place where the Commission has decided that the third country ensures an adequate level of protection.
